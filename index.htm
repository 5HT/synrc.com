Erlang

Erlang was created in 1987 in the Ericsson Computer Science Laboratory. It is the implementation language of such high-load services as Amazon Web Services, Facebook Chat and Ericsson switches. Our mission is to bring simple and manageable solutions based on Erlang/OTP to our customers.
