Erlang

Erlang was created in 1987 in the Ericsson Computer Science Laboratory. It is the implementation language of such high-load services as Amazon Web Services, Facebook Chat and Ericsson switches which is capable of servicing up to 10M active LTE connections.

Products

Our products support millions of online users thanks to Erlang wich is the uncompromising platform for high-load applications. Our main products line is Erlang implementations of the LDAP and SyncML servers.

Services

As an Erlang Consulting company we provide a set of services starting from advice and consulting to a full development cycle according to your existing business process. Our main rule of development could be formulated as “No Bullshit”.

 

Frameworks

Our success would be impossible without the support of well known tools for the Erlang/OTP platform. Thanks to the existence of RabbbitMQ, GProc, Riak, ejabberd, exmpp, Nitrogen, Cowboy, MochiWeb, WebMachine we was able to deliver great solutions to our customers. Our secret is in what are using and how.
