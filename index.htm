Erlang

Erlang was created in 1987 in the Ericsson Computer Science Laboratory. It is the implementation language of such high-load services as Amazon Web Services, Facebook Chat and Ericsson switches. Our mission is to bring simple and manageable solutions based on Erlang/OTP to our customers.

Products

Erlang versions of the LDAP and SyncML servers was our first products. Now we are creating social Feed Server with storage and subscription services along with Fastest Web Famework for Erlang with WebSockets.

Services

As an Erlang consulting company we provide a set of services starting from friendly advice to consulting and full development cycle according to your business. Our main development rule is “No Bullshit”. We have pool of Erlang developers you may contact with directly in case of consulting.
