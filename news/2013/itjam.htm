Конференция IT Jam

Maxim Sohatskiy has spent more than 10 years in enterprise Java/.NET consulting business and has now fully switched to Erlang. He has founded Synrc Research Center in 2005 where he took part in lots of innovative projects. He is an author of N2O — the fastest Erlang web framework for WebSockets, one of Top-30 GitHub Erlang projects. Maxim has publications in fprog.ru, the Russian magazine devoted to functional programming, participated in Erlang User Conference 2012 held in Stockholm, the capital of Erlang. Currently Maxim is a CTO in his company and is working on Voxoz: the first Erlang Platform for web applications done right.
